# Literature Review

## Key Papers

### AI in Architecture
1. **Chaillou, S. (2020).** ArchiGAN: A Generative Approach for Architecture. *Harvard GSD*. — Pioneering GAN-based floor plan generation.
2. **del Campo, M. et al. (2021).** Deep House: GAN-based architectural style transfer. *ACADIA*.
3. **Newton, D. (2019).** Generative design methods in architecture. *International Journal of Architectural Computing*.

### Diffusion Models for Design
4. **Ho, J. et al. (2020).** Denoising Diffusion Probabilistic Models. *NeurIPS*. — Foundation paper for modern diffusion models.
5. **Rombach, R. et al. (2022).** High-Resolution Image Synthesis with Latent Diffusion Models. *CVPR*. — Stable Diffusion foundation.

### BIM + AI Integration
6. **Sacks, R. et al. (2018).** BIM Handbook (3rd ed.). *Wiley*. — BIM methodology reference.
7. **Zhang, J. et al. (2022).** Automated BIM generation from semantic segmentation. *Automation in Construction*.
